Department Background and Vision:

Established in 2016, the Department of Electrical Engineering (EE) at IIT Dharwad is one of the institute's foundational departments. It offers a comprehensive suite of academic programs, including a four-year B.Tech., a two-year M.Tech. (launched in 2024), and two research-intensive programs - M.S. (by Research) and Ph.D. - in Electrical Engineering.

The Department of Electrical Engineering at IIT Dharwad is actively engaged in cutting-edge research across several principal thrust areas:
- Power Systems and Power Electronics, including smart grids, renewable energy systems, and electric vehicles;
- Control Systems, encompassing linear and nonlinear control, robotics, and automation;
- Signal Processing, with focus on image processing, audio/video processing, and biomedical signal analysis;
- Communication Systems, including wireless communications, 5G/6G networks, and IoT;
- VLSI and Embedded Systems, emphasizing digital design, SoC design, and hardware architectures.

The department is deeply committed to fostering innovation and impactful research. It maintains robust collaborations with leading academic institutions, government agencies, and industry partners.
